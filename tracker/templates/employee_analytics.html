Understanding Employee Analytics

This registry and the accompanying visualizations track organization workload distribution and project timelines. Monitoring these metrics prevents team burnout, guides task allocation, and ensures program delivery schedules remain on track.

Active Task Workload

This horizontal bar chart ranks active employees by the volume of tasks currently assigned to them as Pending or In Progress. An excessive concentration of tasks on a single individual indicates potential project bottlenecks and points to re-allocation opportunities.

Task Completion Efficiency

This ratio compares tasks completed successfully against those that lapsed past their deadline as Overdue. A high on-time efficiency rate indicates healthy operational planning, while persistent overdue metrics suggest under-resourcing or unrealistic timelines.
