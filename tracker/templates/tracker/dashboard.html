Understanding Dashboard Analytics

This dashboard summarizes overall meeting activities across thematic programs and operational boundaries. These charts provide key indicators of administrative focus and strategic alignment:

Meetings by Domain

Tracks coordination and meeting frequency across departments. A high concentration in a single domain points to active reviews, training, or field-mobilization events within that specific sector, while lower-density bars suggest areas that may be in self-execution phases.

Meetings by Intervention Scale

Illustrates the operational distribution of our meetings. It balances local/field engagement (Individual & Community) against strategic partnerships (District, State & National), indicating whether efforts are skewed towards grassroots delivery or systemic policy work.
