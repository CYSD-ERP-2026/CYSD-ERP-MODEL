Understanding Scope & Scale Analytics

This analytical dashboard maps the geographic and operational scope of meetings (Scales) against departments (Domains). By examining this intersection, leadership can visualize whether specific domains are primarily focused on localized execution or engaged in high-level strategic alignment.

Local Execution

Individual & Community scales represent direct localized delivery and team mobilization. High volumes here show a strong field presence and citizen/client-centric execution.

Regional Operations

District scale indicators show middle-tier execution, regional capacity building, and alignment with regional operations and agencies.

Strategic Alignment

State & National scales track state-wide reviews, national partnership building, and strategic alignment. This represents system-strengthening and advocacy efforts.
