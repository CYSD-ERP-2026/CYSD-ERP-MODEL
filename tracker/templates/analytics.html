Understanding Policy Intervention Analytics

This analytical dashboard maps the geographic and operational scope of our meetings (Intervention Scales) against our programmatic departments (Thematic Domains). By examining this intersection, leadership can visualize whether specific domains are primarily operational at the grassroots level or engaged in high-level advocacy.

Grassroots Level

Individual & Community scales represent direct programmatic delivery and localized stakeholder mobilization. High volumes here show a strong field presence and citizen-centric execution.

Regional Level

District scale indicators show middle-tier execution, capacity building of local bodies, block-level coordination, and departmental alignment with local government agencies.

Macro Policy Level

State & National scales track state-wide reviews, national coalition building, and legislative policy reviews. This represents system-strengthening and advocacy efforts.
